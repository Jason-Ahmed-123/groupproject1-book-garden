The Book Garden awaits at the edge of imagination. Plant seeds of inspiration, and watch your Book Garden grow.


The Book Garden generates random book recommendations based on what inspires you. Maybe you have a favorite author, or a love of dragons and fairy folk. Perhaps you have a penchant for mystery, or want to learn how to knit a sweater for your iguana. The Book Garden exceeds expectations by adding a touch of randomization to the mix. We trust you will find more than you're searching for.

The Book Garden will soon grow into a community space where you can come and chat with like minded lovers of literature, hear talks from authors, and participate in creative writing workshops.
